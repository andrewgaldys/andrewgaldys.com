Grew up in Palm City, moved to New York at 18. I joined my first founding team at 16 as an AI Workflow Engineer at Nova Echo AI where we built voice agents for small businesses and hit six figures in ARR. That set the tone for everything after.

Since then I have been across AI, real estate, wealth management, social networking startups, and recruiting. I founded Cipher Intel to give business students a real place to publish research and build a name for themselves. Right now I am CoS at Ambees.io, a startup I am building with friends from Georgetown and UC Berkeley, while doing tech recruiting at Venturi in NYC.

Outside of work I am in the markets, big wins on NVDA and Silver among others. I play golf whenever I can and lately I have been teaching myself Python so I can actually build the things I come up with.
